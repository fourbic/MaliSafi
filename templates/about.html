About MaliSafi

Our Mission

MaliSafi uses advanced AI to help you find the perfect property in Kenya. Our name combines "Mali" (property in Swahili) and "Safi" (clean/clear), reflecting our mission to bring clarity to your property search.

How It Works

MaliSafi employs a team of specialized AI agents that work together to find, analyze, and report on properties that match your criteria:

  • Property Researcher: Searches across multiple sources to find properties matching your requirements
  • Market Analyst: Evaluates investment potential and comparative value
  • Location Specialist: Assesses neighborhood trends and geographic insights
  • Report Editor: Compiles all findings into a comprehensive, easy-to-understand report

Our Coverage

We currently cover major Kenyan cities including:

  • Nairobi
  • Mombasa
  • Kisumu
  • Nakuru
  • Eldoret
  • Malindi

With property types ranging from residential apartments to commercial spaces and vacation homes.

Technology

MaliSafi is built using a combination of technologies:

  • CrewAI for multi-agent orchestration
  • Large Language Models for property analysis
  • Web scraping tools for real-time data collection
  • Flask web framework for the application
